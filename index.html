Press the Up arrow key to move forward.

Press the Down arrow key to move backward.

Press the Left arrow key to rotate left.

Press the Right arrow key to rotate right.

Press the Spacebar key to shoot.

Press P to pause or play.

Press R to reset.

Power-ups (circles containing a question mark) activate on their own once acquired (except the teleportation power-up).

The teleportation power-up gives you an extra teleport that you can activate using the Ctrl key.

The invincibility power-up gives you an invincibility ring that can also be used to destroy asteroids on contact while increasing your firing rate.

Avoid asteroids and shoot them to gain points.

Moving over the edge of the screen causes you to teleport back into the viewport through the opposite edge of the screen.

Respawning upon death grants you a brief period of protection.
